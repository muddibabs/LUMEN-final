Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive my report?

Standard plans respond within 5 hours. Premium responds immediately — often within the hour of submission.

What is the personalised skincare PDF?

Every patient receives a PDF generated from their intake responses right when they submit — including a step-by-step morning & evening routine, product recommendations with links, and what to avoid.

What does preferred clinic booking mean?

Premium patients receive a dedicated booking at one of our partnered clinics, at a time and location of their preference — coordinated by our team.

Can I consult from outside Pakistan?

Yes. We serve patients across Pakistan, UAE, the UK and internationally. Prescriptions may vary by location — your doctor will advise.

Is my information kept private?

Absolutely. All patient data, photos and medical history are handled with strict confidentiality and never shared with third parties.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash and international card payments. Payment details are provided after your booking request.
